The Tam Farms Model

Tam Farms is a chain model — not a single location. A replicable system of standards, curricula, governance, and revenue layers that can be applied at any compliant physical site.

What is Tam Farms?

Tam Farms (Những Khu Vườn Tâm) is a chain model, not a fixed location. The model owns methodology, standards, licensing rights, and replication systems. Any physical site that meets the Tam Farms Location Standard can adopt the model and deliver a consistent participant experience, regardless of geography.

This means: Tam Farms does not depend on a single garden, a single house, or a single address. The model can operate in Da Lat today, in Lac Duong next month, and at a partner site in another region in the future — with the same program standards, the same expert standards, and the same governance.

Tam Farms is an expert model — wherever I go, whatever brand, I can follow the standard curriculum of this model.

The model is operated under the legal entity CÔNG TY TNHH SX-TM-DV THÁI LÂM (Tax ID: 5801443073), active since 2020 in Da Lat, Lam Dong. All licensing, contracts, and legal commitments flow through this entity.

5 revenue layers

Tam Farms does not sell a single product or a single location. Revenue comes from five distinct layers, each with its own mechanism and standard.

Layer 1

Location

Revenue from stays, meals, amenities, and on-site services at a certified location.

Layer 2

Program

Revenue from program fees: workshops, cohorts, residencies, project labs, career direction, technology, agriculture.

Layer 3

Expert

Revenue share or fees routed through certified experts: mentoring, consulting, projects, revenue share.

Layer 4

Enterprise

Revenue from enterprise contracts: corporate residency, team programs, innovation labs, outsourcing, brand incubation.

Layer 5

Licensing

Revenue from licensing the Tam Farms model to partner-operated locations: location licensing, program licensing, brand license, QA subscription.

4 equity node types

Each location in the Tam Farms network belongs to one of four equity node types, defining ownership structure and revenue sharing.

Node 1

Founder-owned

Location fully owned and operated by the founder or the company. Full control, highest standard.

Node 2

Partner-owned

Partner owns the asset; Tam Farms provides brand, model, experts, programs, operations, and customers.

Node 3

Joint venture

Tam Farms and partner co-invest capital, assets, and operations; share revenue and equity per agreement.

Node 4

Expert-led

A certified expert develops the location under Tam Farms standards; contributes expertise and operations.

Rule: Equity must never become a public profit promise. All investment requires founder approval.

6 user journeys

Each person comes to Tam Farms on a different journey. The six journeys below define how the model serves each group.

Journey 1

Trial living

Test the ability to live locally, work remotely, and sustain the real rhythm of life before deciding to relocate.

Journey 2

Career orientation

Review your capabilities, choose a direction, build a real project, and create a 30–90 day action plan.

Journey 3

Expert

Share expertise, build programs, test new models, and develop a location under Tam Farms standards.

Journey 4

Founder / entrepreneur

Build a product, a brand, validate the market, and find resources in a real-world environment.

Journey 5

Technology builder

Work on real projects: build apps/websites, automation, and technology for businesses with expert guidance.

Journey 6

Agriculture and local economy

Practice agriculture, develop products, and build local brands from a real garden.
