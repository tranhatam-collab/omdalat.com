For experts

Trần Hà Tâm · Tam Farm · Da Lat, Lam Dong

You are already an expert in your field — an advisor, a senior freelancer, someone who has worked remotely for years, or someone who wants to step away for a while to think about the next career phase. You do not need anyone to teach you your craft. You need a different environment.

Why an expert needs this place

After many years of work, many experts hit the same condition: better at the work, earning more, but also more tired, more doubtful, and with less time to truly think about the next big direction. You do not need a course. You need space — quiet enough, far enough from the usual pace, real enough that the mind can rest and big thoughts can surface.

Tam Farm does not teach experts their craft. It creates the conditions for experts to look back at themselves — and sometimes, that is worth more than any course.

You can come to

  • Truly rest, not fake rest — no constant email, no daily online meetings, no reading industry news every hour. A week to a month of truly stepping away from the running pace.
  • Think about the next phase — what you have done, what you want to do next, what you want to stop, what you want to begin. Time and quiet to think for real, not to think between two meetings.
  • Work remotely in a different environment — bring your current project, work at a slower pace, see whether you work more effectively when the city's pressure is not pushing you.
  • Try a new direction — write a book, build content, test a new field, things you have long had no time for.
  • Have peer conversations — the founder is also someone who has worked in the field for many years. Conversations between people who have been through it, not between teacher and student.

What experts often realize

After a stretch here, many experts realize similar things:

  • They have been following a pace without remembering why they were running.
  • They are better at the work but not happier than 5 years ago.
  • They could do 30% less without the results dropping — if they choose the right work.
  • They want the next phase to be different in quality, not just more in quantity.

What this is not

  • Not a C-level retreat — no facilitator, no strategy design sessions.
  • Not an expert network — no networking events, no member directory.
  • Not an advisory service — the founder can converse, but does not advise you on your career.
