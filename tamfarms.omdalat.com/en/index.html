What is Tam Farms?

A short answer: this is a place where you experience, not a place where you are served.

Tam Farms (Những Khu Vườn Tâm) is a small-scale living and working space in the outskirts of Da Lat. It does not sell vacations, does not sell therapy, does not promise income. It creates a stretch of time and a real environment — so you can test for yourself whether this way of living suits you.

We host a maximum of 01–03 people each month. Each person can stay from 1 week to 3 months. There are no tours, no packaged itineraries. There is a garden, real work, real people, and real questions for you to answer yourself.
