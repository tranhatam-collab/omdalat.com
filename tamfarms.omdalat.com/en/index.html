What is Tam Farms?

A short answer: this is a chain model, not a single lodging location.

Tam Farms (Những Khu Vườn Tâm) is a chain model developing locations for living, learning, working and real-world experience with experts. Tam Farms owns the methodology, location standards, expert standards, program standards, revenue model and replication system. Lily is the first reference location (Reference Location 01) — where the model is demonstrated and validated.

Each Tam Farms certified location has its own capacity, stay duration and intake conditions — verified against the Tam Farms Location Standard. At Lily, current capacity is 01–03 people/month, with stays from 1 week to 3 months. Future locations will have their own conditions per node.
