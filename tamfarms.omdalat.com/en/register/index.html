Register to participate at Tam Farm

Tam Farm · Da Lat, Lam Dong

Tam Farm hosts a maximum of 01–03 people each month at each location, with stays from 01 week to 03 months. Because the scale is small and the level of accompaniment is high, registration is done step by step to ensure fit for both sides.

Important note:

Submitting information does not mean approval. Each application is reviewed individually, and the founder reserves the right to decline a candidate when it appears not to fit the spirit or the current operating conditions.

Registration process

1. Contact via WhatsApp for an initial conversation

Message us on WhatsApp at +84 84 915 3426 to begin the conversation. In this step, we discuss with you:

  • the goals you want to achieve by participating;
  • your intended stay duration (from 01 week to 03 months);
  • the number of people accompanying you (if any);
  • the nature of your current work and your remote work needs;
  • your expectations for the living space, the garden, and the community;
  • your fit with the pace of life in the outskirts of Da Lat.

This step helps both sides form an initial picture before moving on.

2. Two-way assessment

After the initial conversation, we assess fit together based on:

  • your real needs and the location's ability to meet them;
  • the state of accommodation, work space, and the garden at the time of registration;
  • your ability to adapt to the shared living environment and house rules;
  • expectations about outcomes — because this is not a result-guarantee service, not a therapy program, and not a recruitment service.

The two-way assessment is how we ensure you actually benefit from participating, and that the space runs smoothly when hosting you.

3. Confirmation based on availability

If both sides agree to proceed, confirmation is made based on the real availability of each location. The specific conditions — timing, cost, payment terms, and cancellation — are agreed separately for each booking, with no single fixed price list applied to all.

A schedule is only confirmed after full discussion. We do not hold a spot without clear written or message-based confirmation from both sides.

Online form

Currently, the online registration form is not yet open. Registration is done directly via WhatsApp to ensure each application is discussed seriously and fits real conditions.

When the form is ready, we will announce it on the home page and the contact page.

What you should know before registering

  • This is not a hotel, mass-market homestay, resort, or vacation facility.
  • This is not a medical facility, therapy service, or healing program.
  • This is not a recruitment agency and does not guarantee employment or income.
  • This is not a form of investment and does not guarantee profit.
  • Participation is voluntary. Participants are responsible for their own decisions and experience.

Response time

We try to respond within 24–72 hours of receiving the first message. During peak weeks or while accompanying current participants, response time may be longer. If after 05 days you have not received a response, please message once more.
