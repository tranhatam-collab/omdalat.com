Conditions for a location to be announced

Legal

Lawful usage rights or cooperation agreement. Clear address and person responsible for operations.

Safety

Accommodation safety conditions, electricity, water, sanitation, emergency contact information, and incident handling procedures.

Operations

House rules, pricing, benefits, capacity limits, cancellation/rescheduling policy, and verified real conditions.
