How to become a Tam Farms location

A new location joins the network under one of four equity node types, and must pass the Location Standard (20 criteria) before being certified.

Node 1

Founder-owned

Founder or company directly owns and operates. Full control, highest standard.

Node 2

Partner-owned

Partner owns the asset; Tam Farms provides brand, model, experts, programs, operations.

Node 3

Joint venture

Tam Farms and partner co-invest capital, assets, and operations; share revenue and equity.

Node 4

Expert-led

A certified expert develops the location under Tam Farms standards.

Reference: Tam Farms Location Standard (TAMFARMS_LOCATION_STANDARD_2026) — 20 criteria covering legal, safety, operations, house rules, and quality governance.

Important notice

No location is announced until it completes operational verification under the Location Standard.

Every certified location must display the label "A TAM FARMS REFERENCE LOCATION".
