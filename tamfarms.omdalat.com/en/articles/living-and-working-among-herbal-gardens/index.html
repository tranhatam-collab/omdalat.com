Living and working among herbal gardens on the outskirts of Da Lat

A space to live, work, and experience short-term stays among an ecological garden, vegetables, fruits, and natural herbs on the outskirts of Da Lat.

For: People who need a quiet space to work, rest, and restore their rhythm of life

Are you looking for a place quiet enough to work, spacious enough to breathe, and close enough to nature for your body to return to its natural rhythm?

On the outskirts of Da Lat, Tam Farms offers a short-term living and working model within a grounds that has greenery, vegetables, fruits, and natural herbs.

This is not just a place with rooms. It is a living context for you to work more focused, eat more simply, move more naturally, and take time to observe yourself again.

A day can begin very differently

  • Morning: light breakfast, tea or coffee, walking, and tending the garden.
  • Daytime: online work, research, or project development in a private area.
  • Afternoon: harvesting vegetables, cooking, reading, chatting, or joining shared activities.
  • Evening: rest in a low-noise, low-distraction environment.

Who is this space for?

  • Experts, researchers, creators, and remote workers.
  • People who need to complete a project, manuscript, research, or important plan.
  • People who want to experience gardening, clean food, and life close to nature.
  • People tired of noise, heat, or an overly fast pace of life.
  • People who want to restore healthier sleep, eating, movement, and work habits.

Herbs and self-care

The garden may have some seasonal herbs and food plants. Participants can learn to identify, care for, and use them in everyday life with appropriate guidance.

These activities do not replace medical examination, treatment, or consultation with qualified professionals.

Experience duration

  • Minimum: 1 week.
  • Maximum: 3 months.
  • Estimated intake: 1–3 people per month.
  • Registration required 1–2 weeks in advance.
A space does not change your life. But an environment quiet enough can help you see clearly what needs to change.
