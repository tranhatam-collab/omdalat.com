For experts and researchers who need focused space for extended stays

A quiet living and working space for experts, researchers, authors, and people developing in-depth projects.

For: Experts, researchers, authors, lecturers, and advisors

There are tasks that cannot be completed amid constant meetings, noise, and hundreds of notifications every day.

A research project, manuscript, training program, or new strategy needs long enough focus time and a low-distraction environment.

A working residency, not a vacation

Participants can maintain their personal work schedule while having the opportunity to exchange expertise, walk, garden, and join small conversations.

Suitable for completing

  • Writing books, reports, or professional documents.
  • Designing training programs.
  • Research and product development.
  • Preparing workshops, lectures, or strategies.
  • Testing models combining technology, education, and agriculture.

What can experts contribute?

When voluntary and scope is agreed upon, experts can share a talk, critique projects, or guide a small group. Contribution is not automatically an obligation in exchange for accommodation.

Professional boundaries

All consulting, research, or mentoring activities must clearly state scope, responsibilities, and rights to use results.

A good space does not do the work for you. It protects the focus you need to do your most important work.

Estimated experience duration

Minimum 1 week, maximum 3 months. Limited intake each month — register in advance to discuss fit, availability, cost, and specific conditions.
