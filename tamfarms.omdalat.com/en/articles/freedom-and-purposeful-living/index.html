Freedom is not going everywhere

Being finalized · Coming soon

This article is being finalized. Please check back soon.

The content will cover the four layers of freedom:

  • Freedom to choose where to live
  • Freedom to choose how to work
  • Financial freedom in the sense of having active capacity
  • Inner freedom, so as not to live entirely by fear and outside expectations

The goal is to help participants build a workable life structure, not a beautiful image of freedom for social media.
