Privacy Policy

Tam Farm · Những Khu Vườn Tâm · Updated 2026

This page explains how Tam Farm collects, stores, and processes your personal information. Since this is a static website with small-scale operations, our privacy policy is correspondingly simple and transparent.

1. Data we collect

When you contact us to inquire or register, we may collect the minimum information needed to assess fit:

  • name;
  • contact information (phone, WhatsApp account, email if provided);
  • expected duration of stay;
  • needs and expectations you share during the conversation;
  • number of people accompanying you (if any).

We do not collect sensitive data such as bank card numbers, passport numbers, or detailed medical information via messages. Payment information (if any) is handled through a separate channel and agreed upon per booking.

2. How data is stored

Information you share is stored in existing communication channels (WhatsApp conversations, email, or internal documents) and only the founder and operator have access. We do not store registration data on public servers and do not use online databases for this purpose.

Data is retained for as long as needed for registration assessment, program operations, and reasonable internal record-keeping. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized.

3. No third-party sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Information is used internally only for registration assessment and program operations.

The only exception is when required by law or legitimate request from competent authorities.

4. Right to request deletion

You have the right to request that we delete or anonymize your personal information at any time. Send a WhatsApp message to +84 84 915 3426 with your request. We will process it within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is legally required.

5. No tracking cookies

This website is static and does not use tracking cookies. We do not install analytics tools that track user behavior (such as Google Analytics), do not use advertising pixels, and do not track you across other sites.

If this changes in the future, we will update this page and notify clearly before implementing.

6. WhatsApp and third-party policy

When you contact us via WhatsApp, the conversation takes place on WhatsApp's platform. How WhatsApp collects, stores, and processes your data is governed by WhatsApp's privacy policy, not by Tam Farm. We recommend reading WhatsApp's policy to understand how the platform handles data.

7. Links to external sites

This website may contain links to external sites (e.g., WhatsApp, government agencies). Tam Farm is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of those sites. Please review each site's own policy before sharing information.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share, including limiting internal access and not storing sensitive data on public systems. However, no method is completely secure. If an incident involving personal data occurs, we will notify you within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • know what information about you is being stored;
  • request access, correction, or deletion of personal information;
  • decline permission for us to continue storing your information (this will prevent further registration assessment);
  • ask questions about how your information is processed.

10. Contact about privacy

If you have questions or requests regarding privacy, send a WhatsApp message to +84 84 915 3426. We prioritize responding to each specific request.

11. Policy updates

This policy may be updated as needed. The applicable version is the one published on this page at the time you access it. The update date is noted at the top of the page.

Summary:

We collect minimally, do not share with third parties, do not track with cookies, and you can request data deletion at any time.
