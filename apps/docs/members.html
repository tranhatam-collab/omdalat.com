Free Member

The standard entry point

Free Member is how a new person or organization enters OMDALAT without public pricing pressure. It gives OMDALAT the context needed to show the right package lane and the right next step.

What Free Member does

  • Captures role intent and basic context.
  • Routes people into the right package lane.
  • Keeps exact pricing gated until the role is clear.

What Free Member does not do

  • It does not expose public pricing.
  • It does not promise runtime access on its own.
  • It does not replace trust-sensitive review later in the flow.
