Getting Started

The OMDALAT entry flow is intentionally calm. A new person should understand the node, understand the rules, choose a role, and only then enter the member system. Public docs are here to reduce noise, not create pressure.

Step 1: Read the public node

Start on omdalat.com. The public site explains what OMDALAT is, who it serves, and why it exists.

Step 2: Read the docs surface

Use docs.omdala.com to understand roles, trust, programs, and the package presentation rules.

Step 3: Enter through Free Member

Free Member is the standard intake path. It lets the system route you into the right lane before showing exact pricing.

Step 4: Review your role-fit package ladder

Package names and comparison logic are public. Exact pricing appears only after the Free Member step.

What not to expect on the public surface

  • No exact pricing on the public site or public docs.
  • No promise that public copy itself defines runtime access.
  • No one-size-fits-all package lane.
