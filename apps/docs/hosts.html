Hosts

Host entry and host-ready framing

Hosts and place owners should not be dropped into a flat pricing page. They should enter through Free Member, describe their place and operating rhythm, and only then review the host lane with the right package options.

Good public signals

  • Place context and hosting intent.
  • What kind of activity the host can support.
  • Why the place belongs in the city layer.

What stays gated

  • Exact pricing.
  • Detailed unlock logic.
  • Deeper runtime readiness decisions.
