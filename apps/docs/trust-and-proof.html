Trust & Proof

Trust is built through visible proof. Proof is how real actions become meaningful history. Without proof, the system becomes a directory. With proof, the system becomes a trusted local operating layer.

Proof

Proof is the recorded evidence that an action, session, collaboration, or outcome took place.

Verification

Verification determines whether proof can be trusted, weighted, and shown publicly.

Moderation

Moderation prevents low-quality or misleading evidence from distorting the trust layer.

Trust layer

The trust layer helps future users make better decisions by surfacing history, not just claims.
