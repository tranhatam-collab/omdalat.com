Slower living is not laziness

A piece on the difference between intentional slowness and directionless drift.

Slower living only has value when it comes with clarity and discipline. A healthy slower pace lets you observe more carefully, do fewer things but do the right ones, and keep enough space so you do not drive yourself into exhaustion. Without structure, what looks like slowness quickly turns into delay.
