You do not need to move fast

A guide to re-checking your pace instead of following outside pressure blindly.

Faster is not always better. A pace that is steady, clear, and repeatable is what allows you to live with your decisions for the long run. In Dalat this becomes even clearer: if you force yourself to live at your old speed, you quickly feel everything pulling away from your energy and original purpose.
