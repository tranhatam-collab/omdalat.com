## Description (max 4000 characters)

A pocket-sized book of ninety-nine short philosophical cuts. Each page presents the original Vietnamese on the left and the English translation on the right. Not a self-help manual. Not a success map. Not a healing guide. It is a short read, but not a light one. After reading, what remains should not be a conclusion. What remains should be a different quality of stillness.

This bilingual edition preserves the voice and brevity of the original while offering an English mirror for international readers, language learners, and those who read between two tongues.
