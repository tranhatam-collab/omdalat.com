## Selection Rule

- prioritize images that are clearly Da Lat
- mix atmosphere and lived detail
- keep color natural
- avoid overly staged luxury visuals
- prefer images with pine, mist, lake, market, station, slope, and real city texture

## Mandatory Usage Rule

- do not choose an image only because it is beautiful
- Om Dalat must use images that help readers believe Da Lat is a place to live and work for real
- work articles should lean toward desks, movement, concentration, small-space work, or everyday work texture
- stay and life articles should lean toward housing, kitchens, windows, slopes, neighborhood streets, and daily rhythm
- bridge blocks to Ap Dalat should lean toward people, places, and story texture rather than join-flow energy
- each image must include `alt_vi`, `alt_en`, `caption_vi`, `caption_en`
- each image must include source and license log before publish
- one image should not be reused across unrelated intents (work vs stay vs income vs editorial bridge) without explicit review

## Avoid

- tourism postcard imagery
- resort-style luxury imagery
- flower-only or mist-only selections when the page is really about work, income, discipline, or staying long term
- abstract mood images with no clear lived context
