# OMDALAT Brand Architecture

Version: 1.0
Status: approved city brand direction

## 1. Brand role

OMDALAT is a standalone city-layer brand rooted in Da Lat.

It is not a tourism identity and not a decorative local brand.

It is:

- a living intelligence city interface
- a trusted local network of places and people
- a density engine for real activity
- a proof surface for what is actually happening in Da Lat

## 2. Positioning

Primary:

- OMDALAT
- The First Living Intelligence City

Secondary:

- Trusted places, people, and activity in Da Lat

## 3. Brand definition

OMDALAT should represent:

- real hosts
- real places
- real experts
- real communities
- real requests
- real proofs

## 4. What OMDALAT must never become

Do not turn it into:

- a travel site
- a tourism blog
- a local directory with no activity
- a decorative community page

## 5. Correct product lens

OMDALAT is:

- a city operating layer
- a local activation system
- a density and proof engine
- a public and local product surface for trusted coordination

## 6. Key surfaces

The first city layer should highlight:

- active places
- verified hosts
- ongoing requests
- upcoming activities
- trusted local nodes
- visible proofs

## 7. Domain strategy

- `omdalat.com` = city homepage
- `app.omdalat.com` = local city experience

## 8. Design tone

OMDALAT should feel:

- alive
- credible
- local
- premium
- operational

It should feel green, calm, and technologically mature without becoming cold, corporate, or generic.

## 9. Final brand rule

Every OMDALAT page should answer:

- what is active now
- who is real here
- what can happen in the city layer
