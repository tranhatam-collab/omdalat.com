## 3. Phase-2 clusters — 30 articles

### Living in Dalat
Climate expectations, transport, budgeting, routines, seasons and local respect.

### Monthly Stay
Applications, room allocation, house rules, extensions, deposits, privacy and shared living.

### Remote Work
Workspace, connectivity evidence, calls, security, productivity and team collaboration.

### Garden Life
Responsible garden routines, seasons, tools, safety, food claims and documentation.

### International Residents
Stay preparation, temporary residence, insurance, visa-information limits, work separation and culture.

### Digital Work
Task briefs, proof of work, portfolio, AI-assisted work, QA and payment states.

### Brand Factory
Local brand intake, image rights, bilingual content, SEO, verification and release gates.

Each cluster receives 4–5 articles to reach the next 30.

## 4. Roadmap to 100 articles

- 10 canonical foundation articles;
- 30 practical guides;
- 20 resident/mentor interviews with consent;
- 15 project case studies;
- 10 garden and local-life field notes;
- 10 international orientation articles;
- 5 annual evidence/transparency reports.

## 5. SEO rules

- No keyword stuffing.
- No unverified “best homestay”, “cheap”, “organic”, “visa service” or work guarantee claims.
- Use location claims only after address/coordinates are approved.
- Use FAQ schema only for visible questions and answers.
- Use lodging schema only after legal review confirms appropriateness.
- Canonical and hreflang must match real language routes.
- Room pages remain `noindex` until approved and complete.
- Application/account pages are `noindex`.

## 6. Article schema

```text
article_id, slug_vi, slug_en, title_vi, title_en, intent,
verified_claims, evidence_ids, author, reviewer, legal_review_required,
content_vi, content_en, media_asset_ids, internal_links,
seo_title_vi/en, meta_vi/en, schema_type, status, published_at
```

## 7. Editorial gate

```text
brief
→ research
→ VI draft
→ fact/evidence review
→ EN adaptation
→ image-rights review
→ SEO review
→ legal review where required
→ admin approval
→ publish
→ scheduled revalidation
```
