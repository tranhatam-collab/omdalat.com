**A long-term living, working, and learning environment in Lac Duong for people who want to build real value through life, work, and digital projects.**

## III. Business model lock

V2 does not sell by night and does not expose a daily booking calendar.

| Product | Minimum duration | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Weekly Stay | 7 nights | Orientation, quiet living, garden and workspace access |
| Extended Weekly Stay | 14 nights | Deeper routine and optional learning track |
| Monthly Stay | 28 nights or written calendar-month term | Stable long-stay residency |
| Builder Residency | 1–3 months | Structured learning and project building |
| Contributor Residency | Project-defined | Accommodation and project agreements remain separate |

No flash sales, OTA-first strategy, hidden work-for-bed arrangement or automatic job promise.

## VII. International resident truth lock

Lily may provide:

- accommodation information;
- general visa and stay information;
- temporary-residence guidance;
- document checklists;
- referral to eligible legal, immigration or employment partners;
- coordination with a legally eligible sponsor entity when one exists.

Lily may not state that it:

- issues visas;
- guarantees visas;
- guarantees work permits or exemptions;
- guarantees employment;
- permits work merely because a person is staying at Lily.

Entry/stay status and work authorisation are separate. A visa or residence status is not treated by the product as automatic permission to perform local work.

## VIII. Operating and payment entity

The commercial contracting/payment entity for the Om Dalat system is locked as **Công ty TNHH Thái Lâm**, subject to evidence that the entity has the authority, registrations and operational arrangements required for the specific Lily accommodation and service activities.

The website must not imply that corporate identity alone proves lodging, fire-safety, food-service, labour or immigration compliance.

## IX. Domain boundaries

- `lily.omdalat.com`: public Lily brand and application entry.
- `app.omdalat.com/lily/*`: operating workspace.
- `brand.omdalat.com`: Lily case study only.
- `api.omdalat.com/api/lily/*`: API.

Lily-specific H1, room sales, reviews, maps, telephone and contact flow must be removed from the Brand Portal homepage and moved to Lily’s own domain after verification.

## X. Mandatory participation flow

```text
Discover
→ Apply
→ Review
→ Approval / Offer
→ Agreements and payment
→ Participation
→ Weekly review
→ Extend / Exit
```

No applicant bypasses review. No project task bypasses eligibility. No public claim bypasses evidence.

## XI. Public language lock

Permitted:

- “weekly and monthly stay”;
- “application-based residency”;
- “optional learning and project pathways”;
- “visa information and referral support”;
- “opportunities depend on suitability, availability and legal eligibility.”

Blocked without evidence or legal approval:

- “book now” for daily stay;
- “guaranteed job”;
- “guaranteed income”;
- “work visa included”;
- “visa sponsorship guaranteed”;
- “organic/clean certified” without certificate evidence;
- “best”, “leading”, “famous” or equivalent superiority claims.

## XII. Founder truth statement

Lily is not built merely to sell rooms. Lily is built to create a place where people can stay longer, work with greater clarity, learn through practice, and gradually create real value for themselves and the projects they join.

All content, images, programmes, work and legal processes must reflect that truth.
