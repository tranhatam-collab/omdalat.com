This register prevents founder statements, external information and future intentions from being published as verified operating facts.

## 4. Legal assumptions requiring revalidation

- Entry/stay permission and work authorisation are separate.
- Foreign temporary residence must be declared through the current competent procedure.
- Foreign work may require a permit, exemption, sponsor or other evidence depending on role and facts.
- Passport, visa, health and permit data are restricted/sensitive operational data.
- Vietnamese personal-data obligations apply, including Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP unless amended or replaced.

Qualified counsel and competent-authority sources must be checked immediately before the international/work lane launches.

## 5. Public claim states

- `verified`: supported by approved evidence.
- `founder_input`: supplied by Founder, not yet field verified.
- `planned`: intended capability, not live.
- `pilot`: limited controlled operation.
- `live`: active and evidence-backed.
- `suspended`: temporarily unavailable.
- `blocked`: may not be published.

## 6. Revalidation triggers

Revalidate when law changes, property changes, operator/entity changes, room capacity changes, a serious incident occurs, consent is revoked, content is older than its review period, or a complaint challenges accuracy.
