## 6. Copy rules

- Never expose nightly rates, daily availability or OTA-style urgency.
- Use “apply”, “request”, “review” and “residency”; avoid “instant booking”.
- Use “information support” and “referral”; avoid “visa service” unless a qualified provider is separately named and contracted.
- English must adapt meaning rather than translate Vietnamese word-for-word.
- Claims about rooms, internet, garden, café, kitchen, location and accessibility remain unpublished until verified.

## 7. Application form fields

Public form may collect only:

- name;
- email;
- phone/messaging contact;
- nationality/country of residence;
- preferred start window;
- preferred duration;
- purpose: stay / learn / project interest / international support;
- short introduction;
- accessibility or important accommodation note;
- consent to contact and process the application.

Passport, visa, health documents and contracts are uploaded only after authentication in the app.
