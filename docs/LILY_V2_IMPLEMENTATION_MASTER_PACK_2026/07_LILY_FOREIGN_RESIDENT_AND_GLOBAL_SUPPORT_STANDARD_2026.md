# LILY FOREIGN RESIDENT AND GLOBAL SUPPORT STANDARD 2026

**Status:** Operational standard; final legal wording requires qualified Vietnamese counsel review.

## 1. Four separate lanes

### Lane A — Stay only
Accommodation application, identity, lawful stay evidence and temporary-residence process. No work promise.

### Lane B — Learning and observation
Training, orientation and non-work observation. Must not be used to disguise labour.

### Lane C — Personal remote work for an overseas party
Resident uses Lily as accommodation/workspace. Lily does not declare that this activity is automatically lawful; the resident remains responsible for appropriate immigration, tax and legal advice.

### Lane D — Local project work or service
Requires classification and legal review. Where a permit, exemption, sponsor, contract or other authorisation is required, the app keeps work locked until evidence is approved.

## 2. Allowed public language

- general visa and entry information;
- accommodation support;
- temporary-residence guidance;
- document checklist;
- referral to qualified providers;
- sponsor assessment where an eligible entity chooses to assess a role.

## 3. Forbidden public language

- guaranteed visa;
- guaranteed approval;
- “work visa included”;
- automatic sponsorship;
- guaranteed work permit/exemption;
- guaranteed employment or income;
- advice represented as an official government decision.

## 4. Case workflow

```text
Application
→ Nationality/stay-status intake
→ Purpose classification
→ Stay-only / learning / overseas-remote / local-work lane
→ Information pack
→ Document request through secure app
→ Qualified review or referral
→ Decision/evidence record
→ Activity unlock or continued lock
→ Expiry monitoring
→ Revalidation
```

## 5. Required case data

- applicant/resident ID;
- nationality and country of residence;
- passport expiry metadata;
- current/expected immigration status;
- intended duration;
- activity description;
- Vietnam project/entity involved;
- sponsor entity if applicable;
- legal reviewer/provider;
- permit/exemption status;
- expiry dates;
- restrictions;
- evidence references;
- activity unlock;
- case owner and audit log.

Passport files are restricted data and never appear in notification subjects, analytics, public CMS or AI training datasets.

## 6. Temporary residence

The operator must maintain a tested process for declaring foreign temporary residence under the current official procedure. The product stores only the operational evidence and status necessary for the stay; it does not claim that a website submission itself completes the government procedure.

## 7. Sponsor governance

A sponsor entity may be shown only when:

- legal identity is verified;
- relationship to Lily/project is documented;
- role and need are reviewed;
- authorised representative is named;
- required filings and approvals are handled through competent channels;
- no promise is made before an authority decision.

## 8. Legal source register

The build team must revalidate current law before production, including:

- Vietnamese immigration law governing entry, exit, transit and residence of foreigners and its amendments;
- current e-visa and entry rules published by competent authorities;
- current rules for foreign workers, work permits and exemptions;
- current temporary-residence declaration procedure;
- personal data protection rules, including Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP or any replacement/amendment.

## 9. Safety and welfare

International residents receive:

- emergency contacts;
- local orientation;
- medical and insurance reminder;
- house rules in a language they understand;
- complaints route;
- anti-harassment protection;
- interpretation limitations disclosure.

## 10. Final gate

```text
verified sponsor where required
+ qualified role review
+ required permit/exemption or other authorisation evidence
+ valid stay status
+ written work/project agreement
= local activity unlock
```
