## 1. Vision

### Vietnamese

Xây dựng hạ tầng thương hiệu địa phương cho Đà Lạt. Giúp mỗi người dân, hộ kinh doanh, nhà vườn, quán nhỏ, homestay, farm, sản phẩm địa phương và doanh nghiệp sở hữu một thương hiệu số có cấu trúc, có khả năng phát triển dài hạn và có thể tiếp cận thị trường quốc tế.

### English

Build the local brand infrastructure for Dalat. Enable local people, household businesses, farms, cafés, homestays, local products, and companies to own structured digital brands that can grow sustainably and reach international audiences.

---

## 2. Mission

Om Dalat does not build websites. Om Dalat builds brand infrastructure:

- Brand identity infrastructure
- Digital presence infrastructure
- Content infrastructure
- SEO infrastructure
- Verification infrastructure
- International expansion infrastructure

for the entire local community.

---

## 3. Positioning Statement

**For:** Local people, household businesses, farms, cafés, homestays, artisans, cooperatives in Dalat and Lam Dong.  
**Who need:** A structured, bilingual, long-term digital brand instead of relying solely on Facebook, Google Maps, or third-party platforms.  
**Om Dalat Brand System Portal is:** The local brand infrastructure platform.  
**That provides:** Intake, verification, strategy, content generation, SEO, compliance, and publishing workflows.  
**Unlike:** Website agencies, listing directories, booking portals, or marketplaces.  
**We:** Give ownership of digital brand identity back to the local community.

---

## 4. What Brand Portal Is NOT

| Misconception | Reality |
|--------------|---------|
| Agency | We do not charge per website. We build reusable infrastructure. |
| Listing Directory | We do not list brands for discovery. We create owned digital identities. |
| Booking Portal | We do not handle reservations or payments. |
| Marketplace | We do not facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers. |
| Social Media | We do not replace Facebook or Instagram. We complement them with owned presence. |
