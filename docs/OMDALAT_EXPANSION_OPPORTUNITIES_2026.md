# OMDALAT_EXPANSION_OPPORTUNITIES_2026

## Strategic Expansion Suggestions
## Version 1.0
## Date: 2026-04-02

---

# 1. Highest-value expansion directions

## Direction A. Creator residency network
Use OMDALAT to connect hosts, places, and creators for short-term residencies, production stays, and collaborative retreats.

## Direction B. Local AI work network
Create a structured network of AI-assisted local services:
- content production
- research support
- business operations support
- design support
- local translation and adaptation
- digital strategy work

## Direction C. City apprenticeship layer
Pair youth, learners, and early-stage creators with real local experts and spaces.

## Direction D. Place operating system
Offer hosts operational tools for calendars, requests, proof history, trust overlays, and activity modes.

## Direction E. Community operating toolkit
Give communities a better system for activities, requests, proof, and place coordination.

## Direction F. City intelligence reports
Publish periodic city reports based on real activity patterns:
- what types of requests are rising
- which place categories are underused
- which creative sectors are growing
- which skills are most needed

---

# 2. Suggested flagship programs

## Program 1. OMDALAT Creative Work Week
A recurring format for creator work, collaboration, showcases, and skill exchange.

## Program 2. OMDALAT Host Network
A trust-based network of spaces ready for work, learning, gathering, and residency.

## Program 3. OMDALAT Proof Library
A structured archive of real activity, case studies, and city outcomes.

## Program 4. OMDALAT Skill Ladder
A pathway from learning to apprenticeship to paid local work.

## Program 5. OMDALAT City Builder Circle
A curated network of organizers, operators, and place-builders who increase city density.
