## What Tam Farms does NOT need (per ADR-003)

- ❌ PCCC certification (location standard, not model standard)
- ❌ Lodging compliance (location standard)
- ❌ Specific physical address (model can be applied anywhere)
- ❌ Room capacity (location standard)
- ❌ Safety SOP for a specific building (location standard)
