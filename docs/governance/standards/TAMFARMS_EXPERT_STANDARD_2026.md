# TAM FARMS EXPERT STANDARD 2026 / TIÊU CHUẨN CHUYÊ GIA TAM FARMS 2026

> **Standard for experts in the Tam Farms network.**
> **Tiêu chuẩn cho chuyên gia trong mạng lưới Tam Farms.**

- **Document ID / Mã tài liệu:** OMD-GOV-STD-004
- **Version / Phiên bản:** 2026.1
- **Status / Trạng thái:** FOUNDATION PUBLIC
- **Owner / Chủ sở hữu:** Founder, Om Dalat ecosystem
- **Approval / Phê duyệt:** Founder approval required (non-waivable)
- **Parent standard / Tiêu chuẩn mẹ:** OMDALAT BRAND STANDARD 2026, TAM FARMS MODEL STANDARD 2026
- **References / Tham chiếu:** ADR-001 (Unified pipeline), ADR-003 (Tam Farms model vs location)

---

## 0. Foundational Principle / Nguyên tắc nền tảng

> *"Không gọi ai là 'chuyên gia' chỉ vì họ tự khai."*
> *"No one is called 'expert' just because they self-declare."*

Expert status in the Tam Farms network is **earned, verified, and re-certified** — not self-claimed. An expert is someone whose expertise has been assessed against this standard, evidenced, and approved by the founder.

Tư cách chuyên gia trong mạng lưới Tam Farms là **đạt được, được xác minh, và tái kiểm định** — không phải tự khai. Chuyên gia là người có chuyên môn được đánh giá theo tiêu chuẩn này, có bằng chứng, và được founder phê duyệt.

---

## 1. Danh tính và hồ sơ / Identity & profile

### 1.1 Required profile fields / Trường hồ sơ bắt buộc

- Full legal name / Họ tên pháp lý
- Preferred display name / Tên hiển thị
- Canonical expert ID (assigned by the platform)
- Contact channel (operational, not necessarily public)
- Profile photo (consented)
- Short biography (VI + EN)
- Affiliated location(s)

### 1.2 Public profile / Hồ sơ công khai

- An expert's public profile MUST list only verified expertise domains and certified programs.
- No expert may publicly claim affiliation with a location or program that has not certified them.

---

## 2. Chuyên môn / Expertise

### 2.1 Definition / Định nghĩa

Expertise is a demonstrated, evidenced capability in a specific domain. It is not a job title, a degree alone, or years of experience alone — it is the combination of knowledge, applied skill, and documented outcomes.

Chuyên môn là năng lực được chứng minh và có bằng chứng trong một lĩnh vực cụ thể. Nó không chỉ là chức danh, không chỉ là bằng cấp, không chỉ là số năm kinh nghiệm — mà là tổ hợp của kiến thức, kỹ năng ứng dụng, và kết quả được ghi nhận.

### 2.2 Evidence required / Bằng chứng yêu cầu

At least one of:
- Portfolio of applied work with verifiable outcomes.
- Peer or client testimonials with contactable references.
- Certifications, publications, or recognized contributions.
- Demonstrated teaching/facilitation with participant evidence.

---

## 3. Kinh nghiệm / Experience

- Minimum experience threshold is defined per domain by the model standard.
- Experience MUST be documented with time, context, and outcomes — not just duration.
- Gaps or inconsistencies in claimed experience trigger verification.

---

## 4. Lĩnh vực hướng dẫn / Guidance domains

- Each expert MUST declare one or more guidance domains.
- A guidance domain is a specific area the expert is certified to lead programs in.
- Declared domains MUST map to certified programs in the Tam Farms Program Standard.
- An expert may NOT lead a program outside their declared domains.

---

## 5. Phương pháp / Methodology

- Each expert MUST articulate their teaching/facilitation methodology.
- The methodology MUST be compatible with the Tam Farms model methodology (learning by living, expert-guided, evidence-based).
- Methodology MUST be documented and reviewable.

---

## 6. Phạm vi trách nhiệm / Scope of responsibility

- The expert's responsibilities MUST be defined per program: content delivery, participant supervision, assessment, and feedback.
- The expert is responsible for the quality and safety of their program content.
- The expert is NOT responsible for location infrastructure (that is the operating entity's duty), but MUST flag infrastructure issues affecting their program.

---

## 7. Giới hạn chuyên môn / Expertise boundaries

- An expert MUST explicitly state what they are NOT expert in.
- An expert MUST decline to lead programs outside their certified domains.
- Cross-domain claims require separate certification per domain.
- Overclaim of expertise is a non-waivable violation.

---

## 8. Chương trình cung cấp / Programs offered

- An expert may only offer programs certified under TAMFARMS_PROGRAM_STANDARD_2026.
- The list of programs an expert is certified to lead MUST be published on their profile.
- An expert may propose new programs; proposals require certification before offering.

---

## 9. Thời gian tham gia / Time commitment

- Each expert MUST declare their availability (hours/week, seasons, on-site vs remote).
- Time commitments to specific programs MUST be documented in the program record.
- An expert who cannot meet a committed schedule MUST notify the operating entity in advance.

---

## 10. Quy tắc đạo đức / Code of ethics

### 10.1 Core principles / Nguyên tắc cốt lõi

- **Honesty / Trung thực** — no overclaim of expertise, outcomes, or affiliations.
- **Participant welfare / An sinh người tham gia** — participant safety and dignity come first.
- **Respect / Tôn trọng** — respect for participants, staff, location, and community.
- **Evidence / Bằng chứng** — claims and outcomes are evidenced.
- **Non-exploitation / Không bóc lột** — no financial, emotional, or power exploitation of participants.

### 10.2 Prohibited conduct / Hành vi cấm

- Romantic or sexual relationships with current participants.
- Financial arrangements outside the declared income mechanism.
- Use of participant data beyond consented purposes.
- Representing Tam Farms without authorization.

---

## 11. Consent

- Experts MUST obtain informed consent from participants for any activity involving physical, emotional, or data exposure.
- Consent MUST be revocable.
- Consent records MUST be retained per the privacy policy.

---

## 12. Bảo mật / Confidentiality

- Expert MUST maintain confidentiality of participant personal data and disclosures.
- Confidentiality may be breached only to prevent harm (safety, legal mandate), with documented reason.
- Expert MUST NOT use confidential information for personal gain.

---

## 13. Cơ chế thu nhập / Income mechanism

### 13.1 Declared income / Thu nhập khai báo

- Each expert MUST have a declared income mechanism: salary, fee-per-program, revenue share, or combination.
- The mechanism MUST be documented in the expert record and the program record.
- No off-book payments from participants are permitted.

### 13.2 Transparency / Minh bạch

- Participants MUST be informed that expert compensation is handled through the operating entity, not via direct side payments.

---

## 14. Cơ chế cổ phần nếu có / Equity mechanism if any

- An expert MAY hold equity in a location under the "Expert-led location" node type (Equity model §4).
- Equity arrangements MUST be documented and founder-approved.
- An expert's equity does not exempt them from this standard or from periodic re-certification.

---

## 15. Đánh giá người tham gia / Participant evaluation

- Each expert MUST define how participants are evaluated in their programs.
- Evaluation criteria MUST be published before the program begins.
- Evaluation MUST be evidence-based, not subjective impression alone.
- Participants MUST receive their evaluation results.

---

## 16. Tái kiểm định / Re-certification

### 16.1 Cadence / Chu kỳ

- Experts are re-certified on a defined cadence (at minimum annually).
- Re-certification reviews: expertise evidence, participant feedback, ethics compliance, programs offered, and income mechanism.

### 16.2 Failure / Không đạt

- Re-certification failure suspends the expert's right to lead programs.
- Reinstatement requires corrected evidence and founder approval.
- Serious ethics violations result in permanent revocation.
