# TAM FARMS LOCATION STANDARD 2026 / TIÊU CHUẨN ĐỊA ĐIỂM TAM FARMS 2026

> **Standard for individual locations in the Tam Farms network (Lily + future locations).**
> **Tiêu chuẩn cho từng địa điểm trong mạng lưới Tam Farms (Lily + các địa điểm tương lai).**

- **Document ID / Mã tài liệu:** OMD-GOV-STD-003
- **Version / Phiên bản:** 2026.1
- **Status / Trạng thái:** FOUNDATION PUBLIC
- **Owner / Chủ sở hữu:** Founder, Om Dalat ecosystem
- **Approval / Phê duyệt:** Founder approval required (non-waivable)
- **Parent standard / Tiêu chuẩn mẹ:** OMDALAT BRAND STANDARD 2026, TAM FARMS MODEL STANDARD 2026
- **References / Tham chiếu:** ADR-001 (Unified pipeline), ADR-003 (Tam Farms model vs location)

---

## 0. Foundational Clarification / Làm rõ nền tảng

### 0.1 A location is a physical operating site

Unlike the Tam Farms model (which is not a location), a Tam Farms **location** is a physical site where participants stay, learn, and work. A location MUST satisfy physical, safety, and operational requirements that the model itself does not require.

Khác với mô hình Tam Farms (không phải địa điểm), một **địa điểm** Tam Farms là một nơi vật lý nơi người tham gia lưu trú, học tập, và làm việc. Một địa điểm PHẢI đáp ứng các yêu cầu về vật lý, an toàn, và vận hành mà bản thân mô hình không yêu cầu.

### 0.2 Required label / Nhãn bắt buộc

Every certified Tam Farms location MUST be publicly labeled:

> **"A TAM FARMS REFERENCE LOCATION" / "ĐỊA ĐIỂM MẪU THUỘC HỆ NHỮNG KHU VƯỜN TÂM"**

This label must appear on the location's canonical page and in participant-facing materials.

### 0.3 Applicable compliance / Tuân thủ áp dụng

Because a location is a physical site, it MUST satisfy where applicable:
- **PCCC / Fire safety** compliance for the building.
- **Lodging / Dịch vụ lưu trú** compliance if accommodation is offered.
- Local regulations for food preparation, workspace, and public access.

## 1. Chủ thể vận hành / Operating entity

### 1.1 Requirement / Yêu cầu

Each location MUST have a defined operating entity — either the founder entity (CÔNG TY TNHH SX-TM-DV THÁI LÂM, MST 5801443073) or a licensed partner entity.

Mỗi địa điểm PHẢI có chủ thể vận hành xác định — hoặc là pháp nhân founder (CÔNG TY TNHH SX-TM-DV THÁI LÂM, MST 5801443073) hoặc là pháp nhân đối tác được cấp phép.

### 1.2 Equity node type / Loại nút cổ phần

The location must declare its equity node type:
1. Founder-owned location
2. Partner-owned location
3. Joint venture location
4. Expert-led location

---

## 2. Quyền sử dụng địa điểm / Location rights

- The operating entity MUST hold a legal right to use the location (ownership, lease, or license).
- Evidence of the right MUST be on file and verifiable.
- The term of the right MUST cover at least the duration of any marketed program.

---

## 3. Chỗ ở / Accommodation

- If accommodation is offered, it MUST comply with lodging regulations.
- Bed count, room types, and capacity MUST be documented.
- Sanitation, ventilation, and basic comfort standards MUST be met.

---

## 4. Bếp và thực phẩm / Kitchen & food

- A functioning kitchen MUST be available if meals are provided.
- Food sourcing MUST be documented (supplier or self-produced).
- Food safety practices MUST be followed.
- Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, allergies) MUST be accommodatable.

---

## 5. Không gian làm việc / Workspace

- A workspace suitable for the offered programs MUST be available.
- Minimum: seating, tables, power, lighting adequate for the program duration.

---

## 6. Internet

- Internet connectivity MUST be available at the location.
- Minimum bandwidth MUST support the program's stated activities (e.g., remote work, research).
- Downtime response MUST be defined.

---

## 7. Khu học tập / Learning area

- A dedicated learning area MUST be available, distinct from sleeping and kitchen areas where feasible.
- The area MUST support the pedagogical format of offered programs (discussion, workshop, fieldwork).

---

## 8. Khu vườn / Garden

- A garden or agricultural area MUST be present or accessible, consistent with the Tam Farms identity.
- The garden MUST be actively maintained.
- The garden's produce, where used in meals or programs, MUST be documented.

---

## 9. An toàn / Safety

### 9.1 General / Chung

- A first-aid kit MUST be present.
- Emergency contact numbers MUST be posted.
- Basic safety orientation MUST be given to participants on arrival.

### 9.2 PCCC / Fire safety

- The building MUST comply with applicable fire safety (PCCC) regulations.
- Evidence of compliance MUST be on file.

### 9.3 Hazards / Nguy cơ

- Known hazards (water bodies, machinery, animals, terrain) MUST be identified and communicated.
- Mitigation for each hazard MUST be in place.

---

## 10. Sức chứa / Capacity

- Maximum participant capacity MUST be declared and not exceeded.
- Capacity is determined by accommodation, workspace, learning area, and safety constraints.
- Overbooking is prohibited.

---

## 11. Lịch hoạt động / Activity schedule

- A daily/weekly activity schedule MUST be published for each active program.
- The schedule MUST include work, learning, rest, and meal times.
- Deviations from the schedule MUST be communicated to participants.

---

## 12. Nội quy / House rules

- House rules MUST be published and communicated on arrival.
- Rules MUST cover: quiet hours, shared space use, guest policy, substance policy, and respect norms.
- Enforcement and consequences MUST be defined.

---

## 13. Privacy và consent / Privacy & consent

- Participant personal data MUST be handled per a stated privacy policy.
- Photos, recordings, and testimonials REQUIRE explicit consent.
- Participants may withdraw consent and request deletion per policy.

---

## 14. Chuyên gia phụ trách / Assigned experts

- Each active program at the location MUST have at least one assigned expert certified under TAMFARMS_EXPERT_STANDARD_2026.
- The expert's scope, time commitment, and contact MUST be documented.
- No program may run without a certified expert on record.

---

## 15. Chương trình được cấp phép / Licensed programs

- Only programs certified under TAMFARMS_PROGRAM_STANDARD_2026 may be offered at the location.
- The list of licensed programs MUST be published on the location's canonical page.
- Deprecated programs may not be marketed as active.

---

## 16. Pricing

- Pricing for each program and stay MUST be published.
- Pricing MUST include what is and is not included.
- No hidden fees. Any optional add-ons MUST be disclosed.

---

## 17. Cancellation

- A cancellation policy MUST be published.
- The policy MUST cover participant cancellation and location-initiated cancellation.
- Refund terms MUST be clear and honored.

---

## 18. Emergency response / Phản ứng khẩn cấp

- An emergency response plan MUST exist and be known to staff.
- The plan MUST cover medical, fire, natural disaster, and security incidents.
- Nearest medical facility MUST be identified and documented.
- Evacuation route MUST be defined.

---

## 19. Phản hồi người dùng / User feedback

- A feedback mechanism MUST be available to every participant.
- Feedback MUST be reviewed by the operating entity and the assigned expert.
- Feedback trends MUST feed into periodic QA (Section 20).

---

## 20. QA định kỳ / Periodic QA

- Each location is re-assessed on a defined cadence (at minimum annually).
- QA covers all 20 criteria in this standard.
- QA failure results in suspension of the "A TAM FARMS REFERENCE LOCATION" label until reinstated.
- QA results are recorded in the D1 record.
