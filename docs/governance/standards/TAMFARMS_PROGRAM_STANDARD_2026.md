# TAM FARMS PROGRAM STANDARD 2026 / TIÊU CHUẨN CHƯƠNG TRÌNH TAM FARMS 2026

> **Standard for programs in the Tam Farms system.**
> **Tiêu chuẩn cho chương trình trong hệ thống Tam Farms.**

- **Document ID / Mã tài liệu:** OMD-GOV-STD-005
- **Version / Phiên bản:** 2026.1
- **Status / Trạng thái:** FOUNDATION PUBLIC
- **Owner / Chủ sở hữu:** Founder, Om Dalat ecosystem
- **Approval / Phê duyệt:** Founder approval required (non-waivable)
- **Parent standard / Tiêu chuẩn mẹ:** OMDALAT BRAND STANDARD 2026, TAM FARMS MODEL STANDARD 2026
- **References / Tham chiếu:** ADR-001 (Unified pipeline), ADR-003 (Tam Farms model vs location)

## 0. Purpose / Mục đích

This standard defines the minimum requirements for any program offered under the Tam Farms model. It also documents the 6 user journeys, 5 revenue layers, and 4 equity node types that structure the Tam Farms system.

Tiêu chuẩn này quy định các yêu cầu tối thiểu cho bất kỳ chương trình nào cung cấp dưới mô hình Tam Farms. Tiêu chuẩn cũng ghi lại 6 hành trình người dùng, 5 lớp doanh thu, và 4 loại nút cổ phần cấu trúc hệ thống Tam Farms.

## Part A — Program Requirements / Phần A — Yêu cầu chương trình

Each program MUST define and publish all of the following:

Mỗi chương trình PHẢI định nghĩa và công khai đầy đủ các mục sau:

### 1. Tên / Name

- Program name in Vietnamese and English.
- Canonical program ID.
- Version (see §17).

### 2. Mục tiêu / Objective

- The stated outcome the program is designed to produce.
- Must be specific and evidenceable, not vague aspiration.

### 3. Nhóm người phù hợp / Target group

- Who the program is for (age, background, goals).
- Who the program is NOT for (exclusions).

### 4. Đầu vào / Prerequisites

- Required prior knowledge, experience, or certifications.
- Required physical or logistical conditions (e.g., ability to stay on-site).

### 5. Thời gian / Duration

- Total duration.
- Schedule structure (days, weeks, sessions).
- Minimum attendance required for completion.

### 6. Chuyên gia / Expert

- Assigned expert(s), certified under TAMFARMS_EXPERT_STANDARD_2026.
- Expert's role and time commitment in the program.

### 7. Địa điểm / Location

- Certified location(s) where the program is delivered, labeled "A TAM FARMS REFERENCE LOCATION".
- Whether the program is location-specific or portable.

### 8. Hoạt động / Activities

- Detailed activity list.
- Sequence and dependencies.
- Materials and resources required.

### 9. Kết quả đầu ra / Outcomes

- Specific, measurable outcomes.
- What the participant can do/know after completion.
- How outcomes are evidenced.

### 10. Bằng chứng / Evidence

- How participant completion and outcomes are documented.
- Evidence retained per privacy policy.
- Evidence reviewable for QA.

### 11. Giới hạn trách nhiệm / Liability limits

- What the program and operating entity are and are not liable for.
- Participant assumptions of risk where applicable.
- Liability limits MUST be in the participant agreement.

### 12. Giá / Price

- Published price.
- What is included and excluded.
- Optional add-ons disclosed.

### 13. Cancellation

- Participant cancellation terms and refunds.
- Operator-initiated cancellation terms.
- Force majeure provisions.

### 14. Safety

- Program-specific safety requirements.
- Hazard identification and mitigation.
- Emergency response linkage to location plan.

### 15. Privacy

- Participant data collected and purpose.
- Consent requirements.
- Retention and deletion policy.

### 16. Feedback

- Feedback mechanism for participants.
- How feedback is reviewed and acted upon.
- Feedback feeds into program versioning and QA.

### 17. Phiên bản chương trình / Program version

- Every program has a semantic version (MAJOR.MINOR).
- MAJOR changes alter outcomes, structure, or prerequisites — require re-certification.
- MINOR changes alter activities or logistics — require founder notification.
- Deprecated versions remain referenceable but not marketable as current.

## Part B — User Journeys / Phần B — Hành trình người dùng

The Tam Farms system serves 6 distinct user journeys. Each program MUST declare which journey(ies) it serves.

Hệ thống Tam Farms phục vụ 6 hành trình người dùng riêng biệt. Mỗi chương trình PHẢI khai báo hành trình nào mà nó phục vụ.

### Journey 1 — Sống thử / Trial living

- **Who:** People exploring a different lifestyle, considering relocation or career change.
- **Need:** Short, immersive exposure to the Tam Farms environment.
- **Outcome:** Clarity on whether this way of living/working fits them.

### Journey 2 — Định hướng nghề nghiệp / Career orientation

- **Who:** Students, early-career individuals, or career changers.
- **Need:** Structured exposure to possible career paths (agriculture, hospitality, education, entrepreneurship).
- **Outcome:** Informed direction for next steps.

### Journey 3 — Chuyên gia / Expert

- **Who:** Practitioners seeking to become certified Tam Farms experts.
- **Need:** Path to expertise certification and program leadership.
- **Outcome:** Certified expert status (per Expert Standard).

### Journey 4 — Founder/entrepreneur

- **Who:** Aspiring or active founders.
- **Need:** Model understanding, licensing path, or JV exploration.
- **Outcome:** Ability to apply the Tam Farms model at their own location.

### Journey 5 — Technology builder

- **Who:** Builders (engineers, designers, product) contributing to the platform.
- **Need:** Context on the ecosystem to build useful tools.
- **Outcome:** Contributions that serve the ecosystem's standards and pipeline.

### Journey 6 — Agriculture and local economy

- **Who:** Farmers, local producers, cooperatives, local economy actors.
- **Need:** Integration into the Tam Farms supply and education chain.
- **Outcome:** Improved practice, market access, or educational role.

## Part C — Revenue Layers / Phần C — Lớp doanh thu

Tam Farms operates 5 revenue layers. A given transaction may span multiple layers, but each layer's revenue MUST be accounted for separately.

Tam Farms vận hành 5 lớp doanh thu. Một giao dịch có thể trải qua nhiều lớp, nhưng doanh thu mỗi lớp PHẢI được hạch toán riêng.

### Layer 1 — Địa điểm / Location revenue

- Revenue from stays, meals, and on-site services at a certified location.
- Accrues to the operating entity of the location.

### Layer 2 — Chương trình / Program revenue

- Revenue from program fees for structured curricula.
- Shared per the program's declared income mechanism (expert + operating entity).

### Layer 3 — Chuyên gia / Expert revenue

- Revenue share or fees routed to certified experts.
- Defined in the expert's income mechanism and the program record.

### Layer 4 — Doanh nghiệp / Enterprise revenue

- Revenue from enterprise contracts (teams, schools, organizations).
- May include custom programs, bulk stays, or consulting.
- Contracted separately from individual participant transactions.

### Layer 5 — Cấp phép / Licensing revenue

- Revenue from licensing the Tam Farms model to partner-operated locations.
- Includes licensing fees and ongoing royalties per the licensing agreement.

## Part D — Equity Node Types / Phần D — Loại nút cổ phần

Tam Farms defines 4 equity node types for locations. The node type determines ownership, governance, and licensing terms.

Tam Farms định nghĩa 4 loại nút cổ phần cho địa điểm. Loại nút quyết định sở hữu, quản trị, và điều khoản cấp phép.

### Node 1 — Founder-owned location / Địa điểm founder sở hữu

- Fully owned and operated by the founder entity (CÔNG TY TNHH SX-TM-DV THÁI LÂM).
- Full control; all revenue layers accrue to the founder entity (minus expert shares).

### Node 2 — Partner-owned location / Địa điểm đối tác sở hữu

- Owned by an external partner entity.
- Licensed to use the Tam Farms model.
- Licensing revenue (Layer 5) accrues to the founder entity; other layers per agreement.

### Node 3 — Joint venture location / Địa điểm liên doanh

- Co-owned by founder entity and one or more partners.
- Governance and revenue split per JV agreement.
- Founder approval required for all major decisions.

### Node 4 — Expert-led location / Địa điểm do chuyên gia dẫn dắt

- Owned/operated by a certified expert (may hold equity per Expert Standard §14).
- Licensed to use the Tam Farms model.
- Subject to the same location and program standards as all other nodes.

## Part E — Certification & Compliance / Phần E — Chứng nhận & tuân thủ

### E.1 Program certification / Chứng nhận chương trình

- A program is certified when all 17 requirements (§1–§17) are met and founder-approved.
- Certification is recorded in the D1 record.
- Certification must reference the assigned expert(s) and location(s).

### E.2 Periodic review / Đánh giá định kỳ

- Programs are re-certified on a defined cadence.
- Review covers: outcomes evidence, participant feedback, safety record, expert status, and version currency.

### E.3 Non-compliance / Không tuân thủ

- A program failing review is suspended.
- Suspended programs may not be marketed or delivered.
- Reinstatement requires corrected evidence and founder approval.
