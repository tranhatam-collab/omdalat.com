## 0. Foundational Clarification / Làm rõ nền tảng

### 0.1 Tam Farms is a MODEL, not a location

**Tam Farms is NOT a location.** It is a chain model — a replicable system of standards, curricula, governance, and revenue layers that can be applied at any compliant physical site.

**Tam Farms KHÔNG PHẢI một địa điểm.** Nó là một mô hình chuỗi — một hệ thống có thể nhân bản gồm các tiêu chuẩn, chương trình đào tạo, quản trị, và lớp doanh thu, có thể áp dụng tại bất kỳ địa điểm vật lý nào đạt chuẩn.

> *"Tôi tới nơi nào, thương hiệu nào, đều có thể theo khóa chuẩn của mô hình này."*
> *"Wherever I go, whatever brand, I can follow the standard curriculum of this model."*

### 0.2 What Tam Farms does NOT require

Because Tam Farms is a model and not a location, it does NOT require:
- PCCC (fire safety) certification for a specific building.
- Lodging compliance for a specific accommodation.
- A specific registered physical address as the model's identity.

These requirements apply to individual **locations** certified under the Tam Farms Location Standard, not to the model itself.

- **English name:** "Tam Farms" — PLURAL, representing multiple gardens, locations, and experts.
- **Vietnamese name:** "Những Khu Vườn Tâm"

## 1. Tầm nhìn và phương pháp / Vision & methodology

### 1.1 Vision / Tầm nhìn

Tam Farms exists to create a replicable model where agriculture, education, and entrepreneurship converge. The vision is that any compliant location can adopt the Tam Farms curriculum, governance, and revenue layers, and deliver a consistent participant experience regardless of geography.

Tam Farms tồn tại để tạo ra một mô hình có thể nhân bản, nơi nông nghiệp, giáo dục, và khởi nghiệp hội tụ. Tầm nhìn là bất kỳ địa điểm đạt chuẩn nào cũng có thể áp dụng chương trình, quản trị, và lớp doanh thu của Tam Farms, mang lại trải nghiệm nhất quán cho người tham gia bất kể vị trí địa lý.

### 1.2 Methodology / Phương pháp

- **Learning by living / Học qua sống thử** — participants learn by inhabiting a working location.
- **Expert-guided / Dẫn dắt bởi chuyên gia** — every program is led by a certified expert.
- **Evidence-based / Dựa trên bằng chứng** — outcomes are documented, not assumed.
- **Replicable / Có thể nhân bản** — every standard is written so a new location can adopt it.

## 2. Mô hình doanh nghiệp / Business model

Tam Farms operates as a chain model with multiple revenue layers and equity node types. The business is not selling a single product or a single location — it is selling access to a standardized, governed experience that can be delivered at any certified location.

Tam Farms vận hành như một mô hình chuỗi với nhiều lớp doanh thu và loại nút cổ phần. Doanh nghiệp không bán một sản phẩm duy nhất hay một địa điểm duy nhất — nó bán quyền truy cập到一个 trải nghiệm được chuẩn hóa và quản trị, có thể cung cấp tại bất kỳ địa điểm được chứng nhận nào.

---

## 3. Mô hình doanh thu / Revenue model — 5 layers

| # | Layer / Lớp | Description / Mô tả |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Địa điểm / Location | Revenue from stays, meals, and on-site services at a certified location. |
| 2 | Chương trình / Program | Revenue from program fees for structured curricula. |
| 3 | Chuyên gia / Expert | Revenue share or fees routed through certified experts. |
| 4 | Doanh nghiệp / Enterprise | Revenue from enterprise contracts (teams, schools, organizations). |
| 5 | Cấp phép / Licensing | Revenue from licensing the Tam Farms model to partner-operated locations. |

Full detail of the 5 revenue layers is documented in the Tam Farms Program Standard.

---

## 4. Mô hình cổ phần / Equity model — 4 node types

| # | Node type / Loại nút | Description / Mô tả |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Founder-owned location / Địa điểm founder sở hữu | Fully owned and operated by the founder entity. |
| 2 | Partner-owned location / Địa điểm đối tác sở hữu | Owned by an external partner, licensed to use the Tam Farms model. |
| 3 | Joint venture location / Địa điểm liên doanh | Co-owned by founder entity and one or more partners. |
| 4 | Expert-led location / Địa điểm do chuyên gia dẫn dắt | Owned/operated by a certified expert under the Tam Farms model. |

Full detail of the 4 equity node types is documented in the Tam Farms Program Standard.

> *"Không gọi ai là 'chuyên gia' chỉ vì họ tự khai."*
> *"No one is called 'expert' just because they self-declare."*

## 8. Mô hình đào tạo / Training & certification system

### 8.1 Certification tracks / Hệ thống chứng nhận

- **Participant certification / Chứng nhận người tham gia** — issued upon program completion with evidence.
- **Expert certification / Chứng nhận chuyên gia** — issued upon meeting expert standard + re-certification.
- **Location certification / Chứng nhận địa điểm** — issued upon meeting location standard + periodic QA.
- **Operator certification / Chứng nhận nhà vận hành** — issued to entities licensed to run a Tam Farms location.

### 8.2 Curriculum governance / Quản trị chương trình

- All curricula are versioned.
- Curriculum changes require founder approval.
- Deprecated curricula remain accessible for reference but may not be marketed as current.

---

## 9. Quy trình cấp phép / Licensing process

1. **Application / Nộp hồ sơ** — partner submits entity evidence and proposed location.
2. **Due diligence / Thẩm định** — founder entity verifies legal, operational, and fit.
3. **Pilot / Thử nghiệm** — partner runs a pilot program under supervision.
4. **Certification / Chứng nhận** — upon pilot success, location is certified.
5. **Licensing agreement / Hợp đồng cấp phép** — signed, defining revenue share and term.
6. **Ongoing QA / Đánh giá định kỳ** — periodic re-certification.

---

## 10. Quy trình nhân bản / Replication process

1. A new location applies under the licensing process.
2. The location is assessed against the Location Standard (20 criteria).
3. Assigned experts are assessed against the Expert Standard.
4. Offered programs are assessed against the Program Standard.
5. Upon all three passing, the location is certified and added to the public registry.
6. The location is labeled "A TAM FARMS REFERENCE LOCATION".

---

## 11. Quản trị chất lượng / Quality governance

- **Periodic QA / Đánh giá định kỳ** — every certified unit (location, expert, program) is re-assessed on a defined cadence.
- **Participant feedback / Phản hồi người tham gia** — collected and reviewed.
- **Incident review / Rà soát sự cố** — any safety, ethics, or quality incident triggers a review.
- **Founder override / Quyền founder** — the founder may suspend any certified unit at any time for cause.

---

## 12. Dữ liệu và KPI / Data & metrics

### 12.1 Required KPIs / KPI bắt buộc

- Number of certified locations.
- Number of certified experts.
- Number of active programs.
- Participant count and completion rate.
- Revenue per layer (location, program, expert, enterprise, licensing).
- Participant satisfaction score.
- Incident count and resolution time.

### 12.2 Data integrity / Toàn vẹn dữ liệu

- All KPIs are sourced from the D1 record and operational systems.
- No KPI may be publicly reported without source evidence.

## 15. Investment governance

- Any investment into a Tam Farms unit (location, expert equity, JV) requires founder approval.
- Equity node types (Section 4) define the investment structure.
- No external investor may override the non-waivable gates of the umbrella standard.

---

## 16. Risk management

### 16.1 Risk categories / Danh mục rủi ro

- **Operational risk / Rủi ro vận hành** — location failure, expert departure, program quality.
- **Legal risk / Rủi ro pháp lý** — entity compliance, contracts, liability.
- **Reputational risk / Rủi ro danh tiếng** — overclaim, incident, partner misconduct.
- **Financial risk / Rủi ro tài chính** — revenue concentration, licensing default.

### 16.2 Mitigation / Khắc phục

- Diversification across locations and revenue layers.
- Periodic QA and re-certification.
- Contractual liability limits in every program.
- Founder override authority.

---

## 17. Legal framework

- The operating entity (CÔNG TY TNHH SX-TM-DV THÁI LÂM) is the legal backbone.
- Every licensed location must have a contractual relationship with the operating entity or an approved partner entity.
- Participant agreements must reference liability limits defined in the Program Standard.
- All public claims must comply with the no-overclaim gate.

---

## 18. Exit, transfer và valuation / Exit, transfer & valuation

### 18.1 Exit / Thoái vốn

- A founder-owned location may be exited via sale, conversion to partner-owned, or closure.
- A partner-owned location may be exited per the licensing agreement terms.
- Exit requires founder notification and a transition plan for participants.

### 18.2 Transfer / Chuyển nhượng

- Equity in any node type may be transferred only with founder approval.
- Transferee must meet the same standards as the original holder.

### 18.3 Valuation / Định giá

- Valuation is based on: certified location count, active programs, participant throughput, revenue per layer, and brand compliance status.
- A unit that fails non-waivable gates has zero certified valuation until reinstated.
