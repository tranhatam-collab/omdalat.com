## III. FULL CONTENT (COPY DEV DUNG NGAY)

### HERO

- OMDALAT
- He thong thanh pho song cho lam viec, sang tao va tao gia tri thuc
- A Living City System for Work, Creation, and Real Value
- Day khong phai la mot noi de tham quan. Day la noi ban den de lam viec, xay dung va tao ra ket qua thuc.
- This is not a place to visit. This is where you come to work, build, and produce real outcomes.

### OMDALAT LA GI

- OMDALAT la mot he thong thanh pho song thuoc OMDALA, noi con nguoi, dia diem, cong viec va co hoi duoc ket noi thanh cac luong hoat dong co the tao ra gia tri thuc.
- OMDALAT is a living city system under OMDALA where people, places, work, and opportunities are connected into real flows that produce measurable value.
- He thong nay khong phai la nen tang du lich, cung khong phai la mot cong dong don thuan. Day la mot lop van hanh giup moi yeu to trong thanh pho tro nen song, ket noi va co the hanh dong.
- This is not a travel platform and not just a community. It is an operating layer that makes a city functional, connected, and actionable.

### HE THONG HOAT DONG (4 ENGINES)

- Work & Opportunity -> Ket noi con nguoi voi cong viec va co hoi thuc / Connect people with real work and opportunities
- Places & Hosts -> Ket noi dia diem voi nguoi dung va trai nghiem thuc / Connect places with real users and experiences
- Creative Economy -> Bien san pham sang tao thanh gia tri thuc / Turn creation into real value
- Trust & Proof -> Ghi nhan va hien thi ket qua thuc de xay dung niem tin / Record and display real outcomes to build trust

### OMDALAT DANH CHO AI

- Nguoi lam viec tu xa / Remote workers
- Nguoi sang tao va builder / Creators and builders
- Host va chu dia diem / Hosts and place owners
- Cong dong va nhom lam viec / Communities and teams
- Doanh nghiep va to chuc / Organizations and companies

### GIA TRI THUC

- Nguoi dung khong chi tham gia, ma tao ra san pham, cong viec, ket noi va ket qua thuc.
- Users do not just join. They produce work, build projects, create connections, and generate real outcomes.
- Work outputs / Projects / Sessions / Collaborations

### TRUST & PROOF

- Moi hoat dong trong he deu duoc ghi nhan thanh bang chung. Nhung bang chung nay giup tao niem tin va mo ra co hoi tot hon.
- Every action in the system is recorded as proof. These proofs build trust and unlock better opportunities.

### VI SAO LA DA LAT

- Da Lat la noi co mat do cong dong sang tao cao, moi truong song can bang va phu hop de lam viec, xay dung va ket noi.
- Dalat provides a balanced environment with a strong creative community, making it ideal for living, working, and building.
